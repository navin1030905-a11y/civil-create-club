About Club

Building Future Civil Engineers with Vision and Practical Excellence

Civil Create Club is a student-driven academic and activity platform for the Civil Engineering department. It promotes technical learning, teamwork, practical exposure, professional development, and leadership through workshops, site visits, events, achievements, and collaborative activities.

Technical Workshops

Hands-on sessions on structures, transportation, surveying, and practical civil engineering topics.

Site Visit Exposure

Real-world learning from construction practices, project execution, and field engineering methods.

Academic Support

Organized notes, peer coordination, concept support, and a stronger academic environment for students.

Achievements & Leadership

Celebrating innovation, student growth, participation, and departmental pride through visible milestones.
